This Cookie Policy explains how UP Cocreate ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on upcocreate.com. It supplements our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit — like your preferences or actions — to improve your experience.

Similar technologies used alongside cookies include:

  • Web beacons / pixel tags: Tiny transparent images that signal to a server when a page or email has been opened.
  • Local storage: Browser-side storage that persists between sessions without using traditional cookies.
  • Session storage: Temporary storage that is cleared when the browser tab closes.

We refer to all of these collectively as "cookies" throughout this policy.

2. Why We Use Cookies

  • To make the website function correctly;
  • To understand how visitors find and navigate our site so we can improve it;
  • To connect contact form submissions to visitor records in our CRM; and
  • To track the effectiveness of our marketing and outreach activities.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary

Required for the website to function. They do not collect personal information for marketing purposes and cannot be switched off. No consent is required for these cookies.

Analytics

These cookies collect aggregate, anonymized information about how visitors use our site — page views, session duration, and traffic sources. We use this to improve the site. Consent is required in jurisdictions that mandate it.

Marketing & CRM Tracking

Set primarily by GoHighLevel (GHL), our CRM platform, to connect form submissions to a visitor's browsing session and attribute inquiries to their source. This helps us follow up effectively. Consent is required in jurisdictions that mandate it.

5. Third-Party Services

GoHighLevel (GHL)

We use GoHighLevel as our CRM and contact management platform. When you visit our site or submit a contact form, GHL may set tracking cookies to identify your session, link your form submission to your prior web visits, and store your contact details in our CRM. GHL processes this data as our service provider under its Privacy Policy.

To limit GHL tracking, you can clear cookies in your browser or use the opt-out mechanisms in Section 7.

Google Fonts

Our site loads the Plus Jakarta Sans and Inter typefaces from Google Fonts via CSS requests. This sends your IP address to Google's servers but does not set cookies on our site. Google may log the request under its Privacy Policy.

Hosting & CDN

Our hosting provider may collect technical log data (IP address, browser type, referring page) for security and performance purposes.

6. Your Consent

Where required by applicable law — including the EU ePrivacy Directive, GDPR, and UK PECR — we will request your consent before setting non-essential cookies.

Strictly necessary cookies do not require consent. For analytics and marketing cookies, we rely on your consent. You may withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings (see Section 7). Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of any processing that occurred before you withdrew consent.

In jurisdictions where implied consent is legally sufficient, your continued use of the website after being informed of our cookie practices constitutes consent.

8. Do Not Track

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal to websites requesting that they not track you. There is currently no universally accepted standard for responding to DNT signals, and our website does not currently alter its behavior in response to a DNT signal. We will revisit this as standards evolve.

We may update this Cookie Policy when we change the technologies we use, when legal requirements change, or when we update our services. We will update the "Last updated" date and, for significant changes, notify you through the website or by email where we have your contact details.
