Shobha Bagrecha

President

Shobha Bagrecha leads UP Cocreate as President, overseeing client relationships, strategic partnerships, and organizational growth. With deep roots in sustainable enterprise, supply chain management, and cross-cultural business development across Latin America and beyond, Shobha brings a grounded, human-centered approach to building organizations that scale with purpose. Diego Cob

Co-founder · Product Lead

Diego Cob is the Co-founder and Product Lead of UP Cocreate, driving product strategy, digital transformation, and client delivery. With 20+ years of experience across international development, UX design, and digital product management, Diego has led high-impact initiatives at the United Nations Development Coordination Office across 131 countries, designed platforms used by millions through USAGov at Bixal, and built purpose-driven ventures in Costa Rica and the United States. He holds an Executive MPA from NYU and University College London, a BA from Williams College, and holds certifications in PMP, CSM, CSPO, and SAFe methodologies. Diego speaks Spanish, English, and Mandarin.
